43.3%
⚠️ Gift Box Dependency
Gift Boxes contribute 75.8% of total revenue. Any seasonal slump in festive demand could significantly impact overall revenue.
75.8% Rev

Revenue grew from ₹14.0Cr (2021) to ₹13.9Cr (2024), showing stable performance. Q4 consistently peaks due to festival season (Diwali, New Year).
Oct–Dec Peak
📊 Channel Mix
Retail leads with 31.5% revenue share, followed by Wholesale (24.6%) and Online (23.2%). Online channel growing fastest among urban cities.
5 Channels

5.4% Share

2.8% share

Bar category has 8 SKUs but only contributes 8.5% of revenue. Average price of ₹194 is low. Consider premium reformulation or bundle offers.
8.5% revenue

Hazelnut Truffles (6pc) leads individual product margins at 49.1%. Hot Choc Mix (49.2%) and Dark Drinking Choc (49.1%) are high-margin hidden gems.
49.2% peak
🔴 Mint Dark Bar Weakest
Mint Dark Bar has the lowest margin at 34.3% — 10 points below average. Unit cost of ₹129 vs price of ₹196 leaves little room. Review pricing or sourcing.
34.3% margin
🌿 Organic Opportunity

14.9% premium

💡 Channel Insights
🏪 Retail Dominates Volume
Retail leads with 10,207 orders (39.9% of total) and ₹8.58Cr profit. Average order value is ₹19,410 — highest footfall but lower AOV vs Wholesale.
₹8.58Cr
🚛 Wholesale: High AOV
Wholesale has only 3,141 orders but generates ₹6.71Cr profit — Average Order Value of ₹49,268 is 2.5× higher than Retail. B2B bulk orders drive efficiency.
₹49.3K AOV
💻 Online: Best Growth Channel
Online channel delivers ₹6.29Cr profit from 6,362 orders. Lower fulfilment costs and direct-to-consumer approach yield better net margins vs Modern Trade.
₹6.29Cr
⚠️ Modern Trade Pressure
Modern Trade (supermarkets/hypermarkets) contributes 15.1% of profit but faces listing fees, promotions, and return policies that compress margins vs direct channels.
15.1% share
🔴 Export: Huge Untapped Potential
Export generates only ₹1.46Cr from 4 cities — the lowest of all channels. Indian premium chocolate is under-represented in GCC and South-East Asia markets.
₹1.46Cr only

💡 Deep Dive Insights
📊 October is Peak Month
October consistently delivers the highest monthly profit across all years — driven by Diwali gifting. 2021 October alone generated ₹8.36Cr profit, the single highest month.
₹8.36Cr peak
⚠️ June is Weakest Month
June has the lowest average profit across all years — a 35% dip vs annual average. No major festivals in June creates a demand vacuum. Promotions needed.
35% below avg
✅ Consistent Margin
Profit margin has stayed within 43–44% across all 5 years — showing excellent cost discipline and pricing power. COGS has not significantly increased.
43–44% stable
📈 New Year Subcategory #1
New Year subcategory alone generates ₹6.13Cr profit — more than the entire Bars category (₹2.31Cr). A single SKU outperforms an entire 8-product category.
₹6.13Cr
🔴 2025 Data Partial
2025 data only covers Jan–Aug (8 months). Annualised, 2025 is tracking at ₹11.94Cr profit — below 2024's ₹12.04Cr. Q4 festive season will be decisive.
₹11.94Cr (est)

Top combo

B2B pattern
